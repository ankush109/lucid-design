How it works
From request to sign-off in three steps
Ok keeps everyone on the same page without creating another tool your team has to check.
Step 01

Create an approval request

Attach files, context, and a deadline. Assign one reviewer or a full chain — Ok routes it automatically.

Step 03

Approved, rejected, or escalated

Request management
Build any approval workflow, no code required

Sequential and parallel routing

Route to one person at a time or ask multiple reviewers simultaneously. Merge outcomes however you need.

Auto-escalation and reminders

FAQ
Common questions

How is Ok different from just using email or Slack for approvals?

Email and Slack lose context, don't enforce ordering, and leave no traceable record. Ok gives every request a single URL, enforces your routing rules, and logs every decision automatically.

Can reviewers approve without creating an Ok account?

Yes. You can send a magic-link review request to anyone with an email address. They can approve or reject without signing up. Only the requester needs a seat.

Is Ok suitable for compliance-heavy industries?

Ok is used by teams in healthcare, finance, and legal. Enterprise plans include immutable audit logs, SAML SSO, and data residency options in the US and EU.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all requests, decisions, and logs at any time before or after cancellation. Data is retained for 90 days after account closure and then permanently deleted.

Can I build custom integrations?

Yes. Ok has a full REST API and supports webhooks for any trigger or outcome. We also maintain an official Zapier app for no-code integrations.
