FAQ

Answers to common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team replies within 24 hours.

Do I need any equipment to get started?+
No equipment required. During setup, you'll specify what you have access to — whether that's a full gym, a set of dumbbells, resistance bands, or just your bodyweight. PaceForm builds your plan around what you've actually got.
How does the form coaching camera feature work?+
Set your phone up so the camera can see you during your lift. PaceForm uses on-device computer vision to analyze your posture and movement in real time, giving you audio and visual cues mid-rep. No video is stored or sent to our servers — all processing happens locally on your device.
Can I use PaceForm if I'm a complete beginner?+
Absolutely. PaceForm is designed to meet you where you are. Beginners get foundational movement patterns, shorter sessions, and extra coaching cues. As you progress, the AI gradually increases intensity and complexity at a safe, structured pace.
What wearables and health platforms does PaceForm support?+
PaceForm integrates with Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin Connect, Whoop, Oura Ring, Polar Flow, and MyFitnessPal. We're adding Fitbit and Suunto support in Q3 2026.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?+
Yes, cancel anytime from within the app or your account settings. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer prorated refunds for partial months, but your first 14 days are completely free so you can be sure before committing.
Is PaceForm suitable for strength training, cardio, or both?+
Both. PaceForm supports strength training (barbell, dumbbell, machine, calisthenics), HIIT, running, cycling, yoga, and hybrid plans that combine multiple modalities. You can also specify a primary goal — fat loss, muscle gain, endurance, or general fitness — and PaceForm will weight your plan accordingly.
