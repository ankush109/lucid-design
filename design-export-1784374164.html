Frequently asked questions

Does PulseForge only work in dark mode?

Dark is our default because most sessions happen in gyms with harsh lighting or at odd hours. A high-contrast light theme is available in Settings > Appearance.

Which wearables are supported?

Apple Watch (Series 4+), WHOOP 4.0, all Garmin watches from 2019 onward, Oura Ring Gen 3, and Fitbit Sense/Charge 5+. Polar sync is in beta.

Can I import data from Strong, Hevy, or MyFitnessPal?

Yes — we support CSV import from Strong and Hevy, and a direct API bridge with MyFitnessPal for nutrition history.

Is there an Apple Watch companion app?

Yes. The watch app runs standalone during workouts — log sets, start rest timers, and see heart rate zones without your phone.

What if I've never lifted before?

Our onboarding flow assesses experience honestly. Beginners are routed to the "First 90 Days" program with lighter loads and detailed video cues.
